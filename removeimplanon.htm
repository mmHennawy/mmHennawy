How To Remove Implanon

 injection of local anaesthetic 1cm above the distal end of the capsule with 0. 5 - 1 ml of lidocaine 1% with 25 - gauge needle

 advancing the needle to emerge from the skin just near to the other side of the capsule

 fashioning the needle by bent it into a ring around the capsule with mosquito forceps

 making a transeverse incision 2 mm against the lower end of the capsule

 the road pushed down towards the incision and grasped with the index finger and the thumb or mosquito forceps  to be removed
